Discover Nashik

Nashik is a city in the northwest region of Maharashtra, India, situated on the banks of the river Godavari12345. It is the sixth largest city in Maharashtra with a population of around 2.2 million1. Nashik is emerging as one of the fastest-growing cities in India1. The city is known for being one of the Hindu pilgrimage sites, that of Kumbh Mela which is held every 12 years2. Nashik is also known for the production of wine and is one of the major wine-producing regions in India35.
